# Statement of Purpose

I am a Master’s student in Artificial Intelligence with a research-driven focus on **machine learning systems**, **LLMs**, and **graph-based representations**, particularly in the context of **blockchain security and routing**.

My primary interest lies in building **theoretically grounded, system-aware AI models** that operate under real-world constraints: scale, uncertainty, and adversarial settings.

---

## Research Motivation

My work is motivated by a recurring observation: many modern AI systems perform well in isolation but degrade when embedded into complex, multi-agent or multi-source environments.

This has led me to focus on:
- **Graph-structured representations** for reasoning over dependencies
- **LLM-assisted analysis** in security-critical domains
- **System-level failure modes**, not just model-level accuracy

I am particularly interested in problems where correctness, robustness, and interpretability matter more than marginal performance gains.

---

## Current Work

I am currently involved in multiple research efforts, including:
- **Hierarchical graph representations for multi-chain blockchain routing**, with an invited paper accepted at IEEE ICNC 2026
- **LLM-based vulnerability detection for smart contracts and DeFi systems**, as a Research Assistant under Prof. Shucheng Yu
- Ongoing projects at the intersection of **graph learning**, **security**, and **optimization**

Alongside research, I actively build production-grade systems, allowing me to validate theoretical ideas in realistic settings.

---

## Academic Goals

My goal is to pursue a research-oriented career focused on:
- Developing principled AI systems for complex, structured domains
- Bridging theory with deployable machine learning infrastructure
- Contributing to peer-reviewed research while maintaining strong engineering rigor

I aim to continue publishing in reputable conferences and journals, collaborate across disciplines, and work on problems with long-term technical relevance.

---

## Perspective

I value:
- Depth over breadth  
- Clarity over hype  
- Systems over demos  

I am most interested in environments that reward careful thinking, rigorous experimentation, and intellectual honesty.
